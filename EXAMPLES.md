# Exemplos de Antes/Depois para Validação

Este documento contém exemplos reais de diagramação de texto antes e depois do processamento pelo LLM, para validação e aprimoramento contínuo.

## Exemplo 1: Artigos e Parágrafos

### Antes (Problemático):
```
ARTICLE I OBJECTIVES The Contracting Parties shall establish a Free Trade Area in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement and in conformity with the relevant provisions of the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade 1994 (GATT).
```

### Depois (Corrigido):
```
ARTICLE I OBJECTIVES

The Contracting Parties shall establish a Free Trade Area in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement and in conformity with the relevant provisions of the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade 1994 (GATT).
```

## Exemplo 2: Listas Numeradas e Sub-itens

### Antes (Problemático):
```
1. The Parties agree as follows: (a) to maintain confidentiality; (b) to act in good faith; and (c) to resolve disputes amicably.
```

### Depois (Corrigido):
```
1. The Parties agree as follows: 
   (a) to maintain confidentiality; 
   (b) to act in good faith; and 
   (c) to resolve disputes amicably.
```

## Exemplo 3: Espaçamento e Pontuação

### Antes (Problemático):
```
products , services , and goods included in the Agreement .
```

### Depois (Corrigido):
```
products, services, and goods included in the Agreement.
```

## Exemplo 4: Parágrafos Explicativos após Listas

### Antes (Problemático):
```
1. For determining serious injury the following shall be considered. The examination of the impact on the domestic industry concerned shall include an evaluation of all relevant economic factors.
```

### Depois (Corrigido):
```
1. For determining serious injury the following shall be considered. 

The examination of the impact on the domestic industry concerned shall include an evaluation of all relevant economic factors.
```
